Release Notes

What's new in MyPepTracker

v1.2.0 Latest
April 18, 2026
  • Delete logged doses to fix mistakes — vial usage is rolled back automatically so your "doses remaining" estimate stays accurate.
  • Edit an active vial, including the mix date. Useful for correcting reconstitution errors, even on vials flagged as expired.
  • Dose log confirmation — a toast appears after logging and the app returns to the Today screen automatically.
  • Delete peptides with full cascade to vials and dose history.
  • Fixed notification scheduling for fixed-recurring peptides without a set time; reminders now align to your last-dose time.
  • Warning when editing vial concentration would affect previously logged doses.
v1.0.0
April 12, 2026
  • Initial release.
  • Track 3–5 peptides with mixed schedules.
  • Reconstitution logging with auto-calculated concentration.
  • Dose logging with injection site tracking and notes.
  • Backdate doses for past entries.
  • Push notification reminders: dose, overdue, vial expiry, low vial.
  • Flexible scheduling: twice daily through monthly.
  • Cycle tracking with progress and days remaining.
  • 28 peptide presets across 10 categories.
  • Injection site body map visualization.
  • Data export in JSON and CSV.
  • Dose stepper widget with selectable step sizes.
