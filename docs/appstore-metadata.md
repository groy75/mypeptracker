## Description

Track your peptide protocols with precision. MyPepTracker logs reconstitution, calculates concentrations, tracks doses, and reminds you when it's time for your next injection — all privately on your device.

Features:
- Reconstitution calculator with auto-concentration and dose volume in mL and insulin units (IU)
- Reconstitution guide: enter desired doses and get the recommended BAC water volume
- Flexible dose scheduling: twice daily, daily, every other day, weekly, biweekly, or monthly
- Dose logging with injection site tracking, notes, and backdating
- Cycle tracking with progress bar and days remaining
- Push notification reminders for doses, overdue alerts, vial expiry, and low vial warnings
- Injection site body map showing recent site usage
- 28 peptide presets across 10 categories for quick setup
- Export your dose history as JSON or CSV
- 100% offline — no account, no server, no data collection

Your data never leaves your device. No analytics. No tracking. No ads.
