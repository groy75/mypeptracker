Privacy Policy

MyPepTracker — Last updated April 12, 2026

No data collected

Summary

MyPepTracker does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Storage

All data you enter into MyPepTracker — including peptide names, dosing logs, reconstitution records, injection sites, notes, and schedule preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Your data is included in your standard iCloud device backup if you have iCloud Backup enabled on your iPhone. This backup is managed entirely by Apple under their own privacy policy and is encrypted end-to-end.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No account or login required
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting or diagnostics sent to us
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No health data shared with HealthKit or any third-party service
  • No network requests — the app works entirely offline

Notifications

MyPepTracker uses local notifications to remind you about scheduled doses, overdue doses, and vial expiration. These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Data Export

The app allows you to export your dose history as JSON or CSV files. When you export, the file is created locally on your device and shared through the standard iOS share sheet. Where the exported file goes after that is determined by your choice (e.g., saving to Files, sending via Messages, etc.).

Third-Party Services

MyPepTracker does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There are no embedded frameworks that collect data.

Children's Privacy

MyPepTracker is not directed at children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
